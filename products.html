Takoyaki

Our signature Takoyaki is a must-try! These delicious octopus balls are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, topped with savory sauce, mayo, and bonito flakes. Perfect as a snack or appetizer.

Donburi Bowls

Experience the taste of Japan with our flavorful Donburi bowls. Each bowl is a complete meal, featuring a variety of toppings over a bed of steamed rice. Popular choices include Beef Gyudon, Chicken Katsudon, and Tempura Donburi.

Udon

Indulge in our hearty Udon noodles, served in a rich broth with your choice of toppings. Whether you prefer classic Kitsune Udon or spicy Tanuki Udon, our Udon dishes are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Yakisoba

Savor the flavors of Japan with our savory Yakisoba noodles, stir-fried with vegetables and your choice of protein. From traditional pork Yakisoba to vegetarian options, each dish is packed with deliciousness.
